Welcome to Oerth, a campaign setting for 5th Edition Dungeons and Dragons. On this site, you'll find information about the Creator of the setting (a Dungeon Master as you likely are), the setting of Oerth itself, reference maps, and developed lore that you can use to enrich your 5th Edition Dungeons and Dragons homebrew campaign. Feel free to use all or none of the campaign setting resources. The most important part of Dungeons and Dragons is that you enjoy the game as a Dungeon Master and your players are enjoying playing the game.

Thanks for visiting!


King Ugvec Giraudus

Last Updated April 28th

Before Ugvec Giraudus assumed the throne as the first king of Kordholm, he was a half-goliath barbarian, discovering as much as he could about Astvinur. Before his travels, he was a slave. He and his fellow slaves were being transported on a ship leaving the northern region of Aelholt to its southern region when a storm blew their ship off course.

Threatened by starvation and running low on water, the Captain tried his best to rally his crew and find hospitable dry land.
